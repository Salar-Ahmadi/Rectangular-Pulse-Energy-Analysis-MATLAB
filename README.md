
This MATLAB project analyzes a rectangular pulse in both time and frequency domains using Fourier Transform methods.

## Overview

The script generates a rectangular pulse signal and evaluates its energy through:

- Analytical energy calculation
- Time-domain energy computation
- Frequency-domain energy computation using FFT
- Spectral visualization of the pulse
- Energy distribution analysis across frequency ranges

## Features

- Generation of a rectangular pulse with configurable amplitude and duration
- Comparison between analytical and numerical energy results
- FFT spectrum calculation and visualization
- Frequency energy percentage plots for:
  - 0 to 10 Hz
  - 10 to 100 Hz

## Parameters Used

- Amplitude: 7
- Pulse Duration: 3 s
- Total Observation Time: 10 s
- Sampling Frequency: 1000 / T

## Output Graphs

The program produces:

1. Rectangular pulse in time domain  
2. FFT magnitude spectrum  
3. Energy percentage from 0–10 Hz  
4. Energy percentage from 10–100 Hz  

## Purpose

This project demonstrates signal energy analysis and verifies the relationship between time-domain and frequency-domain representations of a rectangular pulse.
